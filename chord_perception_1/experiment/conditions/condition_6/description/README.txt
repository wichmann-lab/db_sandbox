Sound complexity

In this condition, the musical training of the participants varied.

The participants were categorised into three groups based on their musical training:
1) Naive (no current musical engagement, training 2 years) - subject NF
1) Amateur musicians (regular non-professional musical engagement, training <=7 years) - subjects AL, DT, JK, JS
2) Professional musicians (regular professional musical engagement, training <=21 years) - subjects DC, CS
